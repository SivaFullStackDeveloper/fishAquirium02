Crude Fish Oil

Crude fish oil is extracted during the production of fishmeal. It contains a rich mixture of omega-3 fatty acids, particularly EPA and DHA, and is widely used in animal nutrition, aquaculture, and pharmaceutical applications. It helps improve fertility, growth, and immunity.
Benefits: Supports reproductive health, enhances immune function, promotes better feed conversion, and boosts energy due to its high-fat content.
Nutritional Composition: Omega-3 fatty acids (EPA, DHA), unsaturated fats, vitamins A & D, antioxidants.
